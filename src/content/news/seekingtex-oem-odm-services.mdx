At Seekingtex, we understand that every brand has unique requirements. Our OEM/ODM fabric development services help brands create custom textiles that perfectly match their product specifications, performance requirements, and sustainability goals.

## Our Development Process

### Phase 1: Specification

Work with our textile engineers to define your requirements — performance properties, weight, hand feel, color, composition, and certification needs.

### Phase 2: Sample Development

Our R&D team develops lab dips and sample yardage for your evaluation. We typically deliver first samples within 3-5 business days.

### Phase 3: Testing & Approval

Samples undergo comprehensive testing for performance properties, colorfastness, and dimensional stability. We provide full test reports.

### Phase 4: Production

Approved specifications move to bulk production with rigorous in-process quality control at every stage.

### Phase 5: Delivery

Finished goods are inspected, tested, and shipped with complete documentation including test reports and certificates.
