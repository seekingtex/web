Seekingtex has launched an expanded line of eco-friendly fabrics made from 100% recycled polyester (rPET), certified by the Global Recycled Standard (GRS). The new collection includes recycled versions of our most popular fabric types — sun protection, quick-dry, and breathable textiles — all meeting the same performance standards as our virgin material products.

"Demand for sustainable textiles has grown dramatically," said Michael Chen, Sales Director at Seekingtex. "Brands want to reduce their environmental footprint without sacrificing performance. Our recycled fabrics deliver exactly that."

The recycled polyester fabric is manufactured from post-consumer PET bottles, processed into high-quality polyester chips, and spun into yarn. This closed-loop process reduces carbon emissions by approximately 50% compared to virgin polyester production while diverting plastic waste from landfills.

The eco-friendly collection is available now with standard 58/60" width and weights ranging from 100-200 GSM.
