Seekingtex moisture wicking fabrics use multi-channel fiber cross-sections that create capillary channels for rapid moisture transport. The specialized fiber geometry pulls sweat away from the skin to the fabric surface where it evaporates quickly.

Hydrophilic finishing treatments further enhance moisture transport by reducing water droplet surface tension, allowing sweat to spread across the fabric surface for faster evaporation. These finishes are applied during the dyeing process and are durable through 50+ washes.

Moisture management is quantified using AATCC 195 (moisture management tester) which measures wetting time, absorption rate, and one-way transport capacity. Seekingtex fabrics achieve Grade 3-5 (good to excellent) on the overall moisture management capability scale. Applications include sportswear, activewear, base layers, and uniform shirts.
