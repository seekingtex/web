Seekingtex uses fiber-level UV stabilizers integrated during polymer extrusion to create permanent UPF 50+ protection. Unlike topical coatings that wash off, fiber-level UV blockers last the life of the garment.

Advanced weave and knit structures further enhance UV blocking by minimizing gaps between yarns. Combined with special ceramic nanoparticles that absorb and scatter UV radiation, Seekingtex fabrics block 98%+ of UVA and UVB rays.

This technology enables Seekingtex to offer sun protection fabrics for sportswear, outdoor apparel, fashion, and children's wear — all certified to UPF 50+ standards.
