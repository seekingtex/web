Seekingtex antibacterial technology inhibits the growth of bacteria, fungi, and other microorganisms on fabric surfaces. Three primary treatment methods are available depending on durability and performance requirements.

Silver ion (Ag+) technology disrupts bacterial cell membranes and prevents reproduction. Integrated at the fiber level during extrusion, it provides permanent antibacterial protection that lasts 50+ washes. Effective against Staphylococcus aureus and Escherichia coli with >99.9% reduction.

Zinc oxide (ZnO) nanoparticles provide broad-spectrum antimicrobial activity through photocatalytic generation of reactive oxygen species. ZnO is skin-safe, OEKO-TEX certified, and offers additional UV-blocking benefits.

Chitosan, a natural biopolymer derived from chitin, offers a bio-based antimicrobial treatment option. It binds to bacterial cell walls and disrupts membrane function. All treatments are skin-safe and comply with OEKO-TEX Standard 100 requirements.
