Sportswear and athletic training apparel is Seekingtex's primary use case. The moisture-wicking, quick-dry, and 4-way stretch properties of our performance fabrics allow athletes to train in comfort.

Typical applications include running shirts, training singlets, team sports uniforms, yoga wear, and fitness apparel. A moisture-wicking fabric at 120-140 gsm combined with antibacterial protection and UPF 50+ creates the ideal training garment.

Mechanical stretch or spandex-enhanced fabrics provide the freedom of movement needed for dynamic athletic motion.
