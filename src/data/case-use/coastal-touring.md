Tactical and workwear applications demand the highest levels of fabric durability. Seekingtex ripstop nylon/cotton blends at 200-350 gsm provide field-proven tear resistance and abrasion protection.

DWR coating adds water and stain resistance for duty uniforms. Antimicrobial treatment controls odor during extended wear. Mechanical stretch allows freedom of movement without compromising durability.

Applications include law enforcement patrol uniforms, military field gear, industrial workwear, security uniforms, and outdoor tactical equipment.
